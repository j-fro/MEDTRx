MEDTRx helps you and your loved ones keep on top of your medication

Using the MEDTRx pill organizer and free account, you can track whether or not you've taken medication on a daily basis as well as receive notifications if you forget a day.

To get started, register for a free account below. If you already have a MEDTRx Organizer, you can enter the ID (located on a sticker on the device) below to link your account. You can always link your account to a device later in your profile.
